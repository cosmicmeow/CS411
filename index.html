Our Mission


CORSICA is a waitlist management system currently being developed by Old Dominion University's CS410 Red Team. We aim to improve the current CS department's waitlist. CORSICA will provide students and faculty with a friendly web interface which will communicate with the school's central scheduling database. In place of a manual list being keep by advisors, CORSICA will add student to the course's waitlist.


The process is simple. Once the student register with our softwareg, CORSICA will keep track of the list and immediately notify the student once a seat becomes available (via email and text messege, if the student registered their phone number with us). The student will have 24 hours to response. We believe that this will make the registration process easier for both students and faculty and hope to expand the software throughout the university.
